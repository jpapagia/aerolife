About AeroLife

AeroLife is your trusted resource for real-time environmental data. Our mission is to provide accessible and accurate air quality and weather information to empower individuals, organizations, and city planners to make informed decisions for a healthier environment.

What We Do

We utilize cutting-edge technology to aggregate real-time data on air quality and weather conditions from reliable sources like OpenWeatherMap. Our user-friendly web application displays this information through interactive visualizations and easy-to-read metrics.

Why It Matters

Air pollution affects millions of people globally, impacting health, the economy, and the environment. AeroLife bridges the gap between complex data and actionable insights, helping individuals, businesses, and policymakers protect public health and plan for sustainable futures.

Target Platforms

AeroLife is designed to perform seamlessly across modern browsers and devices, including:

  • Desktop Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari
  • Mobile Browsers: iOS Safari, Android Chrome
